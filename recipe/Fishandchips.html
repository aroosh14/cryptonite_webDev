Fish and Chips Recipe

Fish and Chips

A classic British dish featuring crispy battered fish served with golden fries and tartar sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Cod or haddock fillets
  • Flour (for batter)
  • Beer or sparkling water
  • Potatoes (for fries)
  • Tartar sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)
