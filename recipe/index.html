Fish and Chips

A classic British dish featuring crispy battered fish served with golden fries and tartar sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Cod or haddock fillets
  • Flour (for batter)
  • Beer or sparkling water
  • Potatoes (for fries)
  • Tartar sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Buffalo Chicken Pizza

A spicy and tangy pizza topped with buffalo sauce, chicken, and melted cheese for the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Cooked chicken (shredded)
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Ranch dressing (optional)
  • Blue cheese crumbles (optional)
